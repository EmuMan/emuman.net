About the Server

Contrary to its name, 1f1t is a highly collaborative SMP with many active members. There are a few custom items and interactions to enhance the experience, without giving up that vanilla Minecraft feel.

Members can choose to form groups with each other, creating alliances and working together to reach even higher goals. You can grind for obscure resources, build incredible structures, or engage in hand-to-hand combat.

Custom Aspects

1f1t features a number of new items and interactions, including completely unbreakable materials and spawn/respawn charms that enable you to move quickly around the map.

Keep inventory is enabled, which means that you don't have to worry about losing your items when you die.
