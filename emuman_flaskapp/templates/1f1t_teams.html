Teams

What are teams?

Teams are a great way for you to collaborate with like-minded people on building projects, resource gathering, and much more. Each team consists of several people, but it is up to you what you want to do with it.

How do I join one?

Uh, probably message the team owner. You might also have to contact a moderator. This process will probably be streamlined in the future through this website (it is all running on Flask so it's easy to add dynamic features like that).

How do I create one?

I'm not really sure. Probably just message a moderator or something.
